About Our Foundation

PurposeYield Synergy Foundation is committed to transforming lives through education, skill development, and social empowerment. We work with underprivileged communities, particularly children from slums, rural, and tribal areas, to provide them with opportunities for a better future.

As a Section 8 company registered under the Companies Act, 2013, we operate with the vision of creating sustainable social impact through collaborative efforts in education, healthcare, and community development across India.

Our Core Programs

Education for All

Free quality education through schools, learning centers, and libraries focusing on basic and digital education for underprivileged children.

Skill Training

Vocational training programs in tailoring, data entry, digital marketing, and spoken English to enable employment and entrepreneurship.

Social Empowerment

Promoting gender equality, women empowerment, and youth development through community-based education and skill-building activities.
